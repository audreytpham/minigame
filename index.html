Pet Groomer Mini Game

This is a pet grooming mini game based on simulation interface. My simulation shows an example of an event or action that occurs in the real world. There are five different scenes, showing different images and actions. Instructions are provided to show the viewer how to play the simulator. Use keys 1-5 to navigate these different scenes. Press the buttons on the scenes to perform the actions written on the buttons. Three P5 libraries, P5.Scenemanager, P5.Sound, and P5.Clickable, are used to make the user's experience more game-like and enjoyable.
